National Fertilizer Limited.

National Fertilizers Limited (NFL) has been an indispensable part of India's agricultural sector since its inception in 1974. As a leading state-owned enterprise, NFL has consistently met India's fertilizer needs and elevated agricultural productivity, particularly through its focus on nitrogenous fertilizers. The company is deeply committed to enhancing soil fertility and boosting crop yields across the nation, playing a pivotal role in India's journey towards agricultural self-sufficiency. With an expansive distribution network and an unwavering dedication to quality, NFL continues to be a catalyst for agricultural growth in India. By providing farmers with dependable access to high-quality fertilizers and agricultural solutions, NFL not only increases agricultural output but also contributes to the overall prosperity of rural communities across the country. Through ongoing innovation and expansion, NFL remains a trusted partner in India's agricultural development efforts, constantly striving to meet the evolving needs of farmers and the agricultural sector as a whole.
